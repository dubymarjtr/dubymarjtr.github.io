A little bit about me

My background

I was born and raised in Venezuela, where I studied Electronic Engineering for 3 years. Here is where my passion for coding started, C++ being the first programming language I was exposed to. After starting my Junior year, I decided to leave my country and come to the US in 2018. Since then, I have worked with one goal in mind: to finish my degree.

I enrolled in Southwestern Illinois College for the Fall 2020 semester, where I have learned so much from my professors and classmates. I have polished my English and I have developed a love for JavaScript, which is, so far, my favorite programming language. I keep learning new technologies and implementing them in each project I create.

My future

Although the future is very uncertain, I know I want to expand my knowledge and pursue a B.S in Computer Science. I see myself as a web designer/developer with a remote job. The reason why I chose this career is the flexibility it offers. I see myself traveling around the world while working on what I love. I hope to find the right company that provides not only knowledge but values and accomplishments.
